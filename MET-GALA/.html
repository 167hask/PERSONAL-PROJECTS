CELEBRITY STYLE

The Best-Dressed Stars From the 2025 Met Gala

BY CHRISTIAN ALLAIRE

May 6, 2025

SUPERFINE:

tailoring black style

List of best dressed at Met Gala 2025

from my perspective :)

Diljit Dosanjh Brings Punjab to the Red

Carpet

For the Punjabi artist's, Diljit Dosanjh's, first Met Gala appearance, he chose to honor

early Indian dandy.

Rihanna Is Here, and Pregnant!

Hours after A$AP Rocky arrived, Rihanna appeared on the Met steps in a Marc Jacobs suit

perfectly tailored to her baby bump. It was worth the wait.

Bad Bunny looking so Good!

One of my favorite's looks of the night and the hat is just chef's kiss, goes so well with the

overall outfit, love it <3

Dapper Dan

Harlem’s own Dapper Dan, who has said “I went from refashioning fashion to having fashion refashion me.”

Rocky-ing the Carpet!

A$AP Rocky, Vogue May cover star and Met Gala co-chair, has arrived in custom AWGE.It's

his label which had its debut in Paris last June.

Lewis Hamilton looking ethereal

Fresh off the Grand Prix in Miami, one of the 2025 Met Gala co-hosts Lewis Hamilton is here.

His look is custom Wales Bonner and I say he’s definitely in the race for best-dressed tonight.

Colman Domingo

Co-chair Colman Domingo has taken off his cape to reveal a dapper Valentino look.

Teyana Taylor is a Suit Girl

I feel like I need a dedicated guide on all her brooches? This is unequivocal proof

that sometimes when you leave the house it's better to put another thing on instead of taking one off.

Zendaya in a Three-Piece LV Suit Is a 10

Has anyone worn a three-piece suit better? Zendaya in custom Louis Vuitton—with

a brooch at center back—channels a bit of Bianca Jagger’s ’70s glam.

Cardi B’s Look Comes From Across

the Pond

Cardi B chose "Main Character" Burburry suit for the 2025 met gala which dripped-out

tonight in a velvet floor. We have to say we “like it like that.”

That’s So Janelle!

Janelle Monáe Wore a diamond made out of tequila (yes, tequila) to this year's Met

gala 2025. Janelle Monáe wearing Thom Browne only the way that Janelle Monáe can.

Will there be a second outfit reveal? Stay tuned!

Brian Tyree Henry is excellent in Dope Sick.

I've never seen Brian Tyree Henry not give his all on a red carpet and thank god tonight

is no different. He is wearing a look from the Lagos-based brand Orange Culture.

Ayo’s Magnum Opus??

Tonight’s Ferragamo look might be this Opus star’s magnum opus.

Doechiiiii

Doechii is Smoking a Celebratory Cigar on the Carpet for Her First Met in Louis Vuitton

with an LV logo on her cheek—is giving me life!

Alton Mason, What’s the Chest Workout Routine, King?

Model Alton Mason looking incredible tonight!

Pharrell and His Pearly White Jacket!

Pharrell is here—in 100,000 pearls! , artistic director of Louis Vuitton Men,

has arrived at the Met in a fabulous jacket made of beaded pearls—computer, enhance!

Mona Patel back with a bang!

The previous year's look of Mona was just out of this world and this year's look is

amazing as well,but I side with the previous year's look

King Khan on the Red Carpet

Wow! Probably didn't expected to have SRK on the Met Gala, and let's just

say the look didn't disappoint us, wearing a custom sabyasachi look

Mindy Kaling in Harbison

Very on theme and the gown features collar and cuffs with decorative buttons and

long train , adds to the look and makes it look amazing!

It’s Madonna Against the Music!

Madonna is On-Theme in a Crisp, White Tom Ford Suit

Madonna cuts a dashing figure in a Tom Ford Tuxedo at 2025 Met gala

Chappell Roan in the house!

H-O-T-T-O-G-O! Chappell Roan took the lyrics to her song quite literally at tonight’s Met Gala.

Is Public School Back?

Justin Jefferson arrived at the Met Gala with Dao-Yi Chow and Maxwell Osborne video teasing

—is Public School back? It certainly looks like it! The designers posted a cryptic the return

of their label on Instagram today. They said they were “keeping it light,”

but this looks like an announcement to me!

Monica being Monica

This A Complete Unknown star is anything but unknown. Monica Barbaro looks dapper in Dior.

Jennie in your area!

Chanel Ambassador Jennie is Here in—You Guessed it—Chanel!

Always looking sharp as always

Laura Harrier

Laura Harrier wears custom Gap Studio that features a sculpted denim vest with

oversized satin elephant sleeves while the wide legged pants prove that

oversized pants are still in (sorry to those who are wishing for a skinny pant comeback).

Miles Chamley-Watson or “Fencer”

Miles Chamley-Watson or “Fencer” for those who follow him on Instagram is at the Met Gala.

He looks extra dapper—not a surprise for those who witnessed him work his magic

Vogue World: Paris this past June.

Demi Moore is All Tied Up in Thom Browne

I love fun! Demi’s custom Thom Browne is one huge tie—why not?

Regina, King of Everything

How good does Regina King look in this Who Decides War Suit? I love the way the shape of

her hair mimics the shape of the collar.

Damson Idris Is Living Life in the Fast Lane

Damson Idris, star of the upcoming F1 movie, arrived in a racing suit and revealed a dapper Tommy Hilfiger suit.

Everywhere versace?

The legendary designer Donatella Versace brings Chance the Rapper to tonight's Met Gala. The rapper is all “juiced” out in Versace.

Joey, King of Textures

I love how so many people are leaning into textures and prints tonight!

This Miu Miu look on Joey King is definitely one of the hippest of the night.

Not seventeen but enough

S.Coups Looks like a Boss for his Met Debut

Love it!

Jodie Turner-Smith in burgundy

She wore a dramatic burgundy gown with a unique looking hat, and we love every bit of it!

Diaphanous Lupita!

Many guests are opting for a look that juxtaposes masculine and feminine tailoring, and

Lupita’s diaphanous cape really is the best mix of dreamy gown and dandy tailoring.

The jaunty hat is the cherry on top.

Gigi’s Long Locks Match her Gold Dress

Gigi’s look is inspired by the work of Zelda Wynn Valdes,known for work that shaped and

styled the female form (and for designing the Playboy Bunny waitress costumes).

A Sterling Moment

Ugbad Abdi in pinstipes, paisly, and a fedora.
